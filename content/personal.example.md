# Personal Profile Example

Use this file as a template for the private profile you want the chatbot to know.
For a real deployment, copy it to `content/personal.md`, fill in your actual
details, and keep that file local.

## Short professional identity

Write a short, factual summary of the owner: role, years of experience, core
domains, and strongest working stack.

## Career themes

- Describe the kinds of problems the owner likes to solve.
- Mention collaboration style, product judgement, leadership, or technical depth.
- Keep claims grounded in facts you are comfortable making public through chat.

## Contact

- Email: your-public-email@example.com
- GitHub: https://github.com/your-username
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/your-handle

## Chatbot answer guidance

- Prefer concise, grounded answers.
- Do not invent jobs, dates, metrics, contact details, or personal facts.
- If the knowledge base does not contain an answer, say so and offer a next step.
